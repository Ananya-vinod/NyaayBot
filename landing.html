Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and legal matters.

What types of legal services does LegalAssist provide?
LegalAssist offers a comprehensive suite of legal services, including AI-powered document generation for various legal requirements (contracts, agreements, wills, etc.), lawyer consultation services connecting you with qualified legal professionals, AI legal assistant for answering questions and explaining concepts, legal research tools, document review services, and case management. Our platform is designed to address most common legal needs for individuals and small businesses.
How much do the services cost?
We offer various pricing options to suit different needs. Basic document generation starts at ₹499 per document, with subscription plans available from ₹999 per month for unlimited document creation. Lawyer consultations are priced based on the lawyer's experience and specialization, starting at ₹1,500 for a 30-minute consultation. We also offer a free tier that includes access to our AI legal assistant for basic questions and guidance. Detailed pricing is available on our pricing page.
Are the lawyers on your platform qualified?
Yes, all lawyers available for consultation through our platform are verified professionals. We conduct thorough background checks, verify bar council registrations, and evaluate their experience before they can offer services through LegalAssist. Each lawyer profile displays their qualifications, specializations, years of experience, and client reviews to help you make an informed choice. We maintain strict quality standards and regularly review lawyer performance based on client feedback.
Is my legal information secure on your platform?
Absolutely. We implement bank-level security measures to protect your sensitive legal information. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using advanced encryption protocols. We maintain strict access controls, and your documents are stored in secure cloud environments compliant with international security standards. Additionally, our privacy policy ensures we never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. For document generation, you can opt to process data locally in your browser for maximum privacy.
Can LegalAssist handle complex legal situations?
LegalAssist is designed to handle a wide range of legal needs, from simple document generation to moderate complexity legal questions. However, for highly complex legal situations, specialized legal fields, or high-stakes litigation, our platform serves as an excellent starting point but may recommend direct, in-depth consultation with specialized attorneys. Our AI will recognize when a matter exceeds its capabilities and will guide you toward appropriate professional legal help. We aim to complement traditional legal services, providing affordable access to legal assistance while acknowledging the limitations of technology.
